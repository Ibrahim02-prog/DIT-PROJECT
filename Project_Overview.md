# Project Overview

## Introduction

AgriSys is a digital agriculture management platform that provides farmers and agricultural organizations with an intelligent solution for managing every stage of agricultural production.

The platform replaces paper-based farm management with a secure digital ecosystem capable of storing farm information, tracking crop growth, monitoring livestock, managing inventory, scheduling farming activities, generating reports, and connecting farmers directly to agricultural markets.

The Figma prototype demonstrates the complete user experience before software development begins.

---

## Problem Statement

Many agricultural organizations still rely on manual record keeping, resulting in poor data management, inaccurate reporting, low productivity, and communication challenges.

AgriSys addresses these issues by introducing an integrated management platform.

---

## Solution

A centralized cloud-based agriculture management platform featuring:

- Farm registration
- Crop tracking
- Livestock monitoring
- Inventory management
- Marketplace
- Reports
- Analytics
- Notifications
